Toronto Stock Evaluator

By Andrew Yang, Filip Stojanovic & Graeme Ferguson

The Toronto Stock Evaluator provides real-time data analysis concerning the Toronto Stock Exchange.

Use the "Search Ticker" page to search a stock ticker and retrieve a company's data.

Searching tickers: The Evaluator has a search feature that allows the user to find the open, high, low, close, and volume metrics for any company's stocks, as well as the current price of the stock. To find a company's data, follow the steps below:

    Enter the stock ticker of the company into the search bar (ex: AAPL for Apple) Note: This feature is case sensitive. You must enter the ticker in ALL CAPS.
    Be patient. Currently, our system does not use a cache or cookies so the data may take a while to load.
    The name of the corresponding company will appear, along with all six basic metrics. These are calculated on a per-minute basis.
